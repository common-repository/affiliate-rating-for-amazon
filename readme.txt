Create your own Amazon product reviews, link your review with an Amazon Affiliate Link, and earn money to finance your blog.

== Description ==
Create your own Amazon product review and inster it to your blog via shortcode.
 
You can add the shortcode to your blog posts or a page and draw readers' attention to the products you reviewed. 

Simply earn some extra money by having your readers click on your Amazon Affiliate Link.

- Create a pro and a contra list.

- Write a detailed product review.

- Assign a star or percentage rating to the product.
